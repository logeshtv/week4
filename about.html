About This Project

Our Mission

Poetry Collection is dedicated to making classic poetry accessible to everyone. We believe that poetry deserves to be discovered, read, and shared by all. Through our simple yet elegant interface, we connect readers with the timeless works of literary masters.

How It Works

Our website uses the PoetryDB API, a comprehensive public database of classic poetry. This allows us to provide you with instant access to thousands of poems across multiple genres and time periods. The interface is designed to be intuitive and distraction-free, letting the poetry shine.

Getting Started

  1. Browse Authors: Visit the home page and click on any author to see their poems
  2. Search: Use the search box to find poems by title or author name
  3. Explore: Discover new poets and expand your literary horizons
  4. Share: Found a favorite? Share it with friends and fellow poetry lovers

Technical Details

This project was built with clean, semantic HTML and vanilla JavaScript. We intentionally kept the design minimal and readable, focusing on content over flashy effects. The site is fully responsive and works beautifully on all devices.

Questions or Feedback?

We'd love to hear from you! Contact us with your suggestions, feedback, or just to share your favorite poet.
